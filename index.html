Additional Info

Shipping

All orders are shipped via UPS or FedEx; We offer many options with both carriers. Ground shipping can take between 3 to 7 business days for packages to arrive, depending on location.

Shipping costs are based on order, destination, and shipping method. Your final shipping cost is calculated prior to checkout.

Please note: Due to the current pandemic situation orders can take 2-3 weeks to ship out. We apologize for the inconvenience and thank you for your understanding.

Refund Policy

Refund policy We only ship plants that are disease-free and of high quality. That being said, we cannot control what happens to your order during transit. If you receive damaged or incorrect plants, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery of your shipment, so that we may correct the order

Cancellation Policy

We will happily cancel your order if a cancellation request is received before your order is packed. Postponement, cancellation, or change requests may be subject to a restocking fee. Orders that have been packed and are ready to ship, or that have been shipped, are ineligible for cancellation or postponement.
