The Courtyard

Colie's Cactus Courtyard is a family-owned nursery based in Fresno California. We are dedicated to growing a wide variety of cacti and succulents and are constantly adding, developing, and creating more products. We offer the best quality plants at the best prices possible. After all, we've been in the business for years!

Not only are we a nursery that offers beautiful and unique succulent and cactus plants, but we also deliver! We have perfected the art of shipping our gorgeous plants across the country, allowing you to enjoy a new plant, no matter where you are. While it may seem strange to have a plant shipped to you, we ensure that your new plant will arrive in perfect condition. Be sure to shop through our collection of succulents and cacti, read up on care instructions, and order your favorite plant today! Contact us with any questions you may have.
